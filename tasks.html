Task Tracker in JavaScript

The first task is to sign in!

Pictures of the web app

Things you can do with this app right now:

  • Add things you need to do in a simple place!
  • Have access to the tasks anywhere you go with Google login!
  • Remove tasks with the click of a button
  • Sort the tasks by a specific priority (ie. time, difficulty, earliest due date)

Things that are hopefully coming:

  • Add functionality to sync with Google calendar
  • Organizing tasks into user-created groups
  • Keep a history of completed/deleted tasks
